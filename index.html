Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2025

This Chrome extension, Website Time Tracker, helps users monitor how much time they spend on websites. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how we handle your data.

1. What Data We Collect

  • We track time spent on each website locally on your device.
  • No personal information, browsing history, or identity is collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Your data is stored using Chrome's local storage API.
  • It is used only for generating local reports (weekly/daily screen time).
  • We do not share, transmit, or store any data externally.

3. Permissions Used

  • tabs: To know which tab is active for tracking.
  • storage: To store time tracking data and your preferences.
  • notifications: To notify when limit reached.
